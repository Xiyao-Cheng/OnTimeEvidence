The Literature Filter of COVID-19


We provide a Science gateway for researchers who are interested in Covid-19. This website organizes and integrats COVID-19 publications from several journals that are described at the Data Page . You can look through the prepared reports at the Reports page . However, before you use the search part, you need to access first. After you created the account successfully, you can go to the Topic Filter page which let you filter the publication according to the topics and OnEvidence levels.

In addition, we provide a chatbot to help you use our website easily. When you login your account, chatbot will provide personal service for you according to the questionnaire . So once you meet some problems, you can chat with the Chatbot to get customized help in the short time.
